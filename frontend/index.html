Fusion Logic

Weighted confidence aggregation formula

Cfinal = α × CEfficientNet + (1 − α) × CYOLO

where α = 0.65

Rationale: EfficientNet receives higher weight (65%) due to superior feature extraction capabilities for fine-grained classification. YOLO contributes spatial context (35%) to maintain detection awareness.

QA Gate

Images must pass quality checks before entering the pipeline:

  • Minimum confidence threshold: 0.25
  • Minimum bounding box area: 100px²
  • At least one strong detection required

Aggregation Strategy

Final prediction derived through:

  • Majority voting across detected fruits
  • Average confidence of majority class
  • Per-fruit fusion before global aggregation
